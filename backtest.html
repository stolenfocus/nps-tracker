Backtest: Following NPS's Top Buys

What if you bought the 20 stocks NPS was most aggressively accumulating each quarter? This backtest tests exactly that — buying equal-weight positions in the top 20 stocks by share-increase rate, held from one filing date to the next.

Note: This backtest covers US-listed stocks only (via SEC 13F filings). Korean domestic holdings are not included.

Strategy Rules

Strategy: Buy top 20 US stocks where NPS increased shares the most (by dollar invested or share increase rate).

  • 1.Each quarter, identify the top 20 US-listed stocks where NPS increased share count the most (by percentage) vs the previous quarter.
  • 2.Buy equal-weight positions on the first business day after the 13F filing date.
  • 3.Hold until the next 13F filing date + 1 business day, then rebalance.
  • 4.Benchmark against SPY (S&P 500 ETF) over the same holding periods.

Important Disclaimer

This backtest is for educational and informational purposes only. It does not constitute investment advice. Past performance does not guarantee future results. The backtest uses historical 13F filing data and assumes execution at closing prices on the specified dates. Real-world results would differ due to transaction costs, slippage, liquidity constraints, and the 45-day delay between quarter-end and 13F filing deadlines.
