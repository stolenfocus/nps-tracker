Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Who We Are

NPS Tracker tracks Korea's National Pension Service global stock portfolio through publicly available SEC 13F and DART filings. We do not require registration and do not directly collect personal information such as names, emails, or financial data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information directly. However, third-party services integrated into our site may collect certain data automatically:
  • Google Analytics — collects anonymized usage data such as pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and approximate geographic location.
  • Microsoft Clarity — records anonymized session replays and heatmaps to help improve site usability.
  • Google AdSense — may serve personalized or non-personalized ads and use cookies to track ad performance.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies through the third-party services listed above. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns (Google Analytics)
  • Understand user interactions (Microsoft Clarity)
  • Serve relevant advertisements (Google AdSense)
  • Remember theme preference (dark/light mode)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to external websites such as SEC EDGAR and DART. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

Data Retention

We do not store any user data on our servers. All analytics data is retained by the respective third-party service providers according to their own retention policies.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our GitHub repository.
