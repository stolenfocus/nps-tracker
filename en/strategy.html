How It Works

  1. Every quarter, NPS files 13F with SEC (publicly available)
  2. We identify the top 30 stocks where NPS increased investment the most
  3. Filter: only buy stocks with positive 3-month price momentum
  4. Partial rebalance: keep winning positions, replace losers
  5. Repeat quarterly
